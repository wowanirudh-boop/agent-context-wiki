# Refund FAQ

## Questions

Q: How long does a customer have to request a refund?
A: A customer can request a refund within 30 days of purchase.

Q: How many times should the refund worker retry a payment provider call?
A: The refund worker retries failed provider calls 3 times.

Q: What happens after approval?
A: The customer receives an approval notification and the refund is issued.

Q: What happens after rejection?
A: The customer receives a rejection notification with the reason.
